Listings and Requests

How do I create a Task Listing on DashTrade?
Creating a task listing is easy! Simply log into your account and navigate to the "Post a Task" or "Create a Job" section. Fill out the required details such as task description, location, budget, and any specific requirements. Once submitted, tradesmen in the relevant category will be able to view and bid on your task.
How can I ensure quality and reliability when selecting a tradesman for my task?
To ensure quality and reliability, it's recommended to carefully review tradesmen's profiles and ratings. Take the time to read through their reviews from previous clients and check if they have relevant certifications or licenses. Additionally, you can ask for references or samples of their previous work to assess their skills and expertise before making a decision.
What should I include in my task description to attract suitable tradesmen?
A well-written task description can help attract suitable tradesmen. Be specific about the nature of the task, including any necessary skills or qualifications required. Provide details such as the timeline, location, and materials needed. If there are any specific preferences or requirements, mention them clearly to ensure tradesmen have a clear understanding of the job.
