Payments

How can I update my payment information?
To update your payment information, log into your account and navigate to the "Payment Settings" section. From there, you can easily update your credit card details or add a new payment method.
What payment methods are accepted on your website?
We accept a variety of payment methods, including major credit cards such as Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. Additionally, we also support online payment platforms like PayPal and Apple Pay for your convenience.
How can I request a refund?
If you would like to request a refund for a purchase, please reach out to our customer support team with your order details. We will review your request and assist you in the refund process according to our refund policy. Please note that refunds are subject to eligibility criteria outlined in our terms and conditions.
