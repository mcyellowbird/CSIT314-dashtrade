Community Guidelines

What are the Community Guidelines?
We are a varied group of people from many walks of life. That's why we have a set of common rules and behaviours that our members must adhere to in order for everyone to benefit from our community-based marketplace. These are known as the Community Guidelines.

We strive hard to follow these rules not just because we believe in being transparent and honest, but also so that everyone may enjoy our marketplace.

These principles will evolve as we go. We will always be willing to change in order to better serve the DashTrade community as a whole.
What are unacceptable behaviours on DashTrade?
DashTrade does not allow certain unwelcome actions, which may result in the removal of your requests and/or the suspension of your account.

Hatred or Violence
Comments or actions that incite hatred or violence towards specific groups or individuals, including DashTrade representatives, will not be permitted. Racist content or behaviour intended to instigate racism is likewise prohibited.

Discrimincation
Comments that are berating to members of the community or contain vulgarities in the context of a personal attack/insult will not be accepted.

Harrassment
Actions or statements that may create distress or put other members of the community in danger are not permitted. This includes prolonged disruption, agony, intimidation, or communication aimed at influencing the offers of other members, all of which are expressly prohibited.

Illegal Behaviours
DashTrade strongly maintains a zero-tolerance policy for any criminal behaviour, including theft, vandalism, and other illegal activities like as drug or weapon usage.

Trolling
Tasks and remarks perceived to be trolling or improper are not permitted. Off-topic or aggressive comments intended to provoke other members of the community are also not permitted.

What happens if I break Community Guidelines as a Tradesman?
DashTrade is dedicated to creating a welcoming and secure community for everybody. This pledge is based on the ideals of openness and respect.

Our Community Guidelines define the ideals and standards of conduct that we expect from the DashTrade Community. Customers and tradespeople agree to these guidelines and Terms & Conditions when they join DashTrade or create an account.

We treat violations of the Community Guidelines extremely seriously since they ensure a safe and pleasant experience when using the DashTrade platform. Members who violate the Community Guidelines may have their DashTrade account restricted or banned permanently.

What is Subcontracting?
In DashTrade, subcontracting occurs when a task is completed by someone other than the person to whom it was assigned. This sort of agreement is permissible if both the Tasker and the Poster have expressly consented to it.
