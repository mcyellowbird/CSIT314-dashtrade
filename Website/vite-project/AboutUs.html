What is DashTrade?
DashTrade is a unique online platform that connects clients and traders. Our website is meant to help clients identify the proper craftsman for their specific assignment, such as plumbing and electrical services, as well as painting and building. Our mission is to make it easier to discover trustworthy tradespeople, and we accomplish this by properly screening and authenticating all tradespeople on our platform. Clients may save time and money by connecting with qualified tradespeople quickly and effortlessly, with the confidence that they are working with trustworthy specialists, by utilising DashTrade.
Who will I be Working with?
It's entirely up to you! When looking through tasks, you can look at profiles, reviews, and recommendations to see who you'll be working with.
How much does it Cost?
The Total cost of the task will be depended on the chosen company. Following completion of the task, a Service Fee will be deducted from the agreed-upon offer price to cover all Insurance, Handling, and Transaction costs. There are no additional fees or charges.
Is there Insurance?
The DashTrade Insurance Policy from our Insurance business, subject to all policy terms and restrictions, covers the client for their responsibility to third parties for bodily injury or property damage when doing particular job activities.
