Contact Us

There are many answers available on our Support Page, but if you need additional help with a
specific question, we've got you covered!

To obtain help as soon as possible, please follow these steps:

  • Choose the right form for your enquiry. This allows your ticket to be directed to the
    appropriate team.
  • Enter the email address associated with your DashTrade account.
  • Please include as much details as possible. If you want assistance with a task,
    include task data such as the work title or the Tradesman/customer's name.
Type of Enquiry/Request Form
Account Related Account Form
Task related Task Form
Payment Related Payment Form
Other General Form

We will respond within 1-2 business days. If you haven't received our response by then,
please check your junk/spam folder.
